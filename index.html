Why I chose RMIT

There are several reasons to explain why I chose RMIT. Firstly, RMIT can create a fully multinational environment. To define, RMIT's students use English as a language to study and communicate. They prepare for their future career path when they have to do an IT job they have to speak English as well as reading documents written in English. At RMIT, I can stay in my country, and I think it much more confident for me.
